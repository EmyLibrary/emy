Contact App

Main goal of this app is to show how standalone mode works on iOS devices.

To add this app to your homescreen:
- tap the middle button in the Safari bar (at the bottom of your screen)
- Choose "Add to homescreen"

You're done! This app will now behave just like a native one.

This app is also offline capable. Update 'contacts.manifest' version number to update it
